About Me

Hello! I'm JudeBen, a passionate developer and student at Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT) in Kerala, India. This blog is where I share my journey through the world of technology, coding experiences, and life as a computer science student.

Living in the beautiful state of Kerala, I'm constantly inspired by the blend of tradition and technology that surrounds me. Here, you'll find my thoughts on programming, web development, college life, and the exciting projects I'm working on.
